TECHNOLOGY PROJECT

WIC(Wine Intensive Course) Project in Sookmyung Women's University

Our team invented "Feet pressure application"
If you put pressure on the panel, it will recognize the pressure and send the information to your smartphone.
In prototype, we used the hand but in real model, the feet will be there so you can know the fact that which part of your feet receives more pressure.
You can use this information when you buy shoes or make your own shoes.



As you can see, the graph shows the pressure of your feet and you can receive the information on your smartphone.
We used Aduino kit and pressure sensor for this experiment.
